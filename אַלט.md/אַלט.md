אַלט
'old' 

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Got. alþeis, ON ellri 'older', OE eald, English old, OS ald, OHG alt, MHG alt, NHG alt < PGmc. *alda- < PIE *h₂el-tó- (DRV); an adjective continuing the original participle of the verb *alan- 'to grow, to bring up'; cf. Latin altus 'high', Old Irish alt 'heiht, cliff'
{Kroonen 2013:20}
Vowel 11, Proto-Yiddish *a
{Katz 1982: 58}
The idiom די אַלטע גוטע צײַטן 'the good old days', cf. Polish stare dobre czasy.s
