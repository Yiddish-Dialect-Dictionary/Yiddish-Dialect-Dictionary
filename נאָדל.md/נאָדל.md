ETYMOLOGY
===========
Vowel 12, Proto-Yiddish *ɔ̄
{Katz 1982: 59}
The idiom אַ נאָדל נישט דורכצוּוואַרפֿן '(no space) to put in an needle (i.e., cramped)' appears to be a calque of Polish szpilki nie ma gdzie wcisnąć/wsadzić/wetknąć.
{Fliciński 405}
