ETYMOLOGY
===========
The phrase שלאָגן זיך קאָפּ אין וואַנט 'bang your head against the wall (i.e., to try to do something without success)' appears to be a calque of Polish bić głową w mur (or variants walić głową o mur, bić głową w ścianę etc). 
{Fliciński 21, 444}
The idiom רעדן צו דער וואַנט 'to talk to a brick wall (i.e., in vain)' appears to be a calque of Polish gadać / mówić jak do ściany. 
{Fliciński 107}
The idiom צודריקן צו דער וואַנט 'to push to the wall (i.e., compel to do something)' appears to be a calque of Polish pryzcisnąć / przyciskać kogoś do muru.
{Fliciński 335}
