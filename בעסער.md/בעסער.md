בעסער

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Got. batis, ON betr, OE bet, bet(e)ra, OS bat, betera, OHG baz, bezziro, MHG baz, bezzer ENHG bass, besser < PGmc. *batiz 'better' < PIE *bʰHd-is; cf. Sanskrit bhadrá- 'auspicious, happy, pleasant', Younger Avestan hu-baδra- 'very happy'
Adverb to the comparative of *gōda- 'good' (see גוט).
{Kluge/Seebold 2011: 114; Kroonen 2013: 55}
