ETYMOLOGY
===========
{How did the meaning change to 'river'? The meanings in MHG and ENHG don't mention it. Why would it displace פֿלוס?}
ON díki 'swamp, puddle', OE dīc 'dike, ditch', English dike, ditch, OS dīk 'pond', Dutch dijk 'dam', MHG tîch 'pond, swamp, channel', NHG Teich 'pond, pool' < PGmc. *dīka- 'dam; ditch' < *dīkka- < PIE *dʰeiǵʰ-nó-; cf. Greek τοῖχος 'wall'.
Related to *dī̆gan- (see טייג).
