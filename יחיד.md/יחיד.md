Jooched {HOLLAND, Beem, Jerosche, №404}
	•	E ben jooched sol man scheien (Var. meien), a bas jechiede sol man freien. 'An only son one should shun/avoid, an only daughter one should try to marry. The only son is spoiled, the only daughter is the only heiress.'
Bernst. 32 and 43.
Zivy 25 - An anzig Madle doll {soll?} mr fraie, an anziger Jung zoll mr scheie.
	•	Kaal sol nit uf e jooched warten. 'The community is greater than an individual.' {Jerosche №411}
