ETYMOLOGY

וועלן 1
'will (future auxiliary); to want'

Got. wiljan, ON vilja, OE willan, OS willian, wellian, OHG wellan, NHG wollen < PGmc. weljan- 'to want' < PIE uelh₂-; cf. Latin volō, velle, Lithuanian pa-vélti
[Feist 563, Kroonen 2013: 578]
