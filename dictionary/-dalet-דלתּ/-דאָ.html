דאָ
'here'

Got. þar 'there', ON þar, OE þār, þǣ̆r, English there, OS thā̆r, OHG dā̆r(a), MHG dār, dā NHG da 'there' < PGmc. þar < PIE to-r; from the root to- 'that, those' with the locative suffix -r
[EWA 2, 533, Feist 490, Kroonen 2013: 534]
