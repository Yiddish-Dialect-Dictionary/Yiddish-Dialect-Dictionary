באָניפֿראַטן
'hospital in Warsaw, or the street on which it was located'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Named after the Catholic religious order known as the Hospitaller Order of the Brothers of Saint John of God, in Polish known as bonifratrzy, from Latin boni fratres 'good brothers'. 
{Herzog 1964: 85}
