נידעריק
'low, short'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
The meaning 'short' appears to be a calque of Polish niski 'low, short'. 
Vowel 31, Proto-Yiddish *i {but WY vowels appears to be long!}
{Katz 1982: 58}
