ETYMOLOGY
===========
Turkic.
{Beider 2015: 441}
The idiom גיין מיט דער טאָרבע 'to go with the bag (i.e., to go begging)' appears to be a calque of Polish pójść / iść z torbami; an even closer match (because with singular of 'bag') is Belarusian з торбай ісці.
