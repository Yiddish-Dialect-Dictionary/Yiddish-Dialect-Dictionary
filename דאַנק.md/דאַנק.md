ETYMOLOGY
===========
The preposition אַ דאַנק 'thanks to' seems to be a calque of Polish dzięki. 

Got. þagks*, OE þanc, OS thank, OHG danc < PGmc. *þank-a-; probably back-formation from Got. þagkjan 'think'
