ETYMOLOGY
===========
The idiom האַלטן די האַנט אויפֿן דפֿק 'to keep one's hand on the pulse (i.e., keep an eye on)' appears to be a calque of Polish trzymać rękę na pulsie.
{Fliciński 427}
