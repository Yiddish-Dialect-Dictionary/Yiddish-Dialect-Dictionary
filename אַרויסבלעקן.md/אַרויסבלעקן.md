אַרויסבלעקן
אַרויסגעבלעקט
'to stick out (one's tongue)'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
MHG blecken 'to show' (see אַנטפּלעקן); cf. NHG (die Zähne) blecken 'to bare one's teeth'.
{Herzog 1964: 89}
