פּראַווען
געפּראַוועט

{CYED}
celebrate; practice, observe; conduct, carry out, engage in; plaster, roughcast (wall)

ETYMOLOGY
===========
{Perhaps from Ukrainian ра́вити rather than Polish prawić, since 'to do, perform' is a common meaning for Ukrainian, while the Polish cognate means 'to discourse, declaim'.}
