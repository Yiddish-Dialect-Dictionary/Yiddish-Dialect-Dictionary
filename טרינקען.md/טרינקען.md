
טרינקען
געטרונקען
'to drink'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Got. drigkan, ON drekka, OE drincan, English to drink, OS drinkan, Dutch drinken, OHG trinkan, MHG trinken, NHG trinken < PGmc. *drinkan-  or *drenk-a- < *dʰré-n-g-e- (NEUR), nasalized variant of the root *dʰreg- 'to pull' ; cf. Lithuanian drė́gti 'to become moist', drė́gnas 'humid, wet'
{DW¹ 22: 554; Feist 125; Kluge/Seebold 2011: 930; Kroonen 2013: 103}
