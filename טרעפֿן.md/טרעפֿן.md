'to meet; to hit'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
ON drepa 'to strike, beat, knock; to kill, slay', OE drepan 'to strike', OHG treffan 'to hit', MHG treffen, NHG treffen 'to hit, touch' < PGmc. *drepan- 'to hit' (DRV) or *drep-a-, a back-formation to *drupp/bōn- 'to hit' (English to drub) < *dʰrbʰ-néh₂-, which is itself a zero-grade iterative to *draban- 'to hew' (Got. gadraban) < *dʰrobʰ-eie-, from the pre-Germanic root *dʰrebʰ- (see טריפֿן)
{DW¹ 21: 1586; Kluge/Seebold 2011: 927; Kroonen 2013: 101}
