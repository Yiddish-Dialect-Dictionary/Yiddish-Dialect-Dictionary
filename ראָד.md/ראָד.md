ETYMOLOGY
===========
MHG rat, rades; OHG rad; from the Proto-Indo-European root *rot(h)- 'wheel,' from which also Latin rota.
The idiom דאָס פֿינפֿטע ראָד צום וואָגן 'the fifth wheel on a wagon (i.e., unnecessary)' may be a calque of Polish piąte koło u wozu. 
{Fliciński 285}
