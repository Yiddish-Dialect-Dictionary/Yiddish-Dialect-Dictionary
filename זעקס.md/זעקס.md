זעקס
'six'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
{When the original /x/ (as in MHG sehs) change to /k/?}
MHG sehs; OHG sehs; cf. Got. saíhs; ON sex; OE si(e)x; OS sehs; from PGmc. *sehs; from PIE *(s)weḱs; cf. Sanskrit ṣáṣ, ṣáṭ (<*saṭṣ); Greek ἕξ; Latin sex; Lithuanian šešì; Russian шесть.
The PIE intitial s was probably adopted from *septm (see זיבן).
{Burrow 260; Kluge/Seebold 2011: 837; Kroonen 2013: 431; Orel 322}
