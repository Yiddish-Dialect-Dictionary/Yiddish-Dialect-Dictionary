אָפּוואַשן
אָפּגעוואַשן
(ᴠᴇʀʙ)

{CEYD}
wash, pf., wash down (clean), was off, swab (with mop) אָ֜פּוואַשן

ETYMOLOGY
===========
The idiom אָפּוואַשן זיך די הענט פֿון 'to wash one's hands of (i.e., to take no responsibility for)' might be a calque of Polish umywać / umyć ręce.
{Fliciński 437}
