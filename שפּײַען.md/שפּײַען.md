שפּײַען
געשפּיִען/געשפּיגן
'to spit'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
The phrase שפּײַען עמעצן אין פּנים 'to spit in somebody's face' appears to be a calque of Polish  pluć/plunąć komuś w twarz. 
{Fliciński 291}
