ETYMOLOGY
===========
Since Polish adopted rygiel from MHG rigel (from OHG rigil; from Latin regula) one might expect a palatalized /gʲ/ to appear in Yiddish sometimes.
{Brückner 471; Kluge/Seebold 2011: 767}
