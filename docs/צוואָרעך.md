דער

'cottage cheese, curd'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Old Czech tvaroh (perhaps through MHG).
Beider discusses the shift of the initial consonants from /tv/ to /tsv/, influenced by neighboring German dialects in Bohemia. 
{Beider 2015: 429}
