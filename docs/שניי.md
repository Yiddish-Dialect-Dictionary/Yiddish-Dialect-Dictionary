שניי
־ען
דער
'snow'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Got. snaiws, ON snær, snjór OE snāw, OS OHG snēo < PGmc. *snaigw-a-z / *snaiw-a- < PIE *snoi̯gʷʰ- ; cf. Lithuanian sniẽgas, OCS sněgъ
{Bammesberger 1990: 52}
The idiom אַרן עמעצן ווי דער פֿאַראַיאָריקער שניי 'to concern someone like last year's snow (i.e., not at all)' appears to be a calque of Polish coś obchodzi kogoś jak zeszłoroczny śnieg.
{Fliciński 253}
