געטאָ
־ס
די
'ghetto'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
From the area in Venice called "getto" (named after the iron foundries there) where the first compulsory ghetto for Jews was instituted in 1516.
{M. Weinreich, Geshikhte, 3: 162}
