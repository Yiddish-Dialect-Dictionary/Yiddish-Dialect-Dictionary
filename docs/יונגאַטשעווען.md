יונגאַטשעווען
'to bully'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
A mainly Germanic root (see יונגאַטש) with the Slavic suffix -eve-. The verbal suffix ־עווע־ is derived from the Polish suffixes -owa-, -ewa-, -ywa-, -iwa-.
