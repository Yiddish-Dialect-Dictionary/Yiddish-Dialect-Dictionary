שטערן 3

'forehead'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
The idiom האָבן אַ קופּערנעם שטערן 'to have a copper forehead (i.e., impudence, shamelessness)' appears to be a calque of Polish mieć miedziane czoło. Biblical, from Isaiah 48:4 וּמִצְחֲךָ֖ נְחוּשָֽׁה.
{Fliciński 188}
