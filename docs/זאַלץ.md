זאַלץ
'salt'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Got. salt, ON salt, OE sealt, OS salt, OHG salz; high grade derivation from Got. saltan < PGmc. *salta- 'to spice up' < PIE *sal-deh₃, cf. Lithuanian saldùs 'sweet,' Latin sallō 'to salt,' sal 'salt,' Greek ἅλς
{Casaretto 77}
Vowel 11, Proto-Yiddish *a
{Katz 1982: 58}
