צוזאַמען
צוזאַמעט 
'together' (adverb)

ETYMOLOGY
===========
The idiom האַלטן זיך צוזאַמען 'to hold together (i.e., to form a tight group, bonded by friendship)' appears to be a calque of Polish trzymać się razem.
{Fliciński 428}
