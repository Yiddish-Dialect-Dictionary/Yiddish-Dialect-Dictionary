טײַער
'dear; expensive'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
ON dýrr, OE dīere, dēore, English dear, OS diuri, Dutch duur, OHG tiuri, MHG tiure, tiuwer, NHG teuer < PGmc. *deurja- 'precious, valuable, expensive' (GM); perhaps a vr̥ddhi-gerundive 'mournable' to MHG betûren 'to cost, have difficulties with' (NHG bedauern, see באַדויערן).
{DW¹ 21: 367; Kluge/Seebold 2011: 915; Kroonen 2013: 94}
