גאַלגן

גאַלדן {variants}

ETYMOLOGY
===========
{The usual word in Yiddish is תּליה.}
Got. galga 'stake, pole for crucifixion, gallows', ON galgi 'gallows', OE gealga, English gallows, OS galgo, Dutch galg, OHG galgo, galga 'gallows, cross', MHG galge, NHG Galgen 'gallows; boom' < PGmc. *galgan- 'stake?' (or *galgōn) < *ǵʰolgʰ-eh₂- (NEUR) 'stick'.
Related: Lithuanian žalgà 'long, thin stake'.
{DW¹ 2011: 1167; Kluge/Seebold 2011: 328; Kroonen 2013: 164; Orel 124}
