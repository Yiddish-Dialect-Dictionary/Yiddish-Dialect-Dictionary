שלאָפֿן
געשלאָפֿן
'to sleep'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Got. slepan, OE slǣpan ~ slāpan, OS slāpan, OHG slāfan < PGmc. *slēpanaⁿ
{Ringe & Taylor 11}
Vowel 12, Proto-Yiddish *ɔ̄
{Katz 1982: 59}
The comparison שלאָפֿן ווי אַ געהרגעטער 'to sleep like a dead man (i.e., soundly)' appears to be a calque of Polish spać jak zabity. 
{Fliciński 378}
