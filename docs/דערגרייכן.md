דערגרייכן
'to reach'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Patterned on Polish dosięgnąć 'to reach', with the equivalency of the Yiddish prefix דער־ and Polish do- established by verbs like דערגרייכן 'to reach' (Polish dogonić).
