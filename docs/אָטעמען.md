'to breathe'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
{How to explain Central Yiddish עטעמען?}
Herzog suggests that NEY {atəmən} is a late (19th century?) borrowing from NHG.
CY {etəmən/ejtəmən} seems to derive from a different MHG variant: ætemen, instead of âtemen.
{Herzog 1964: 275, 278}

See אָטעם.
