ETYMOLOGY
===========

Related: Greek éxĩnos 'hedgehog, sea-urchin' (which looks like a derivative from exis 'viper'. 
OS igil, OHG ī̆gil, NHG Igel (see איגל) < PGmc. *egila- 'hedgehog, sea urchin' < PIE *h₁eǵʰ-i-lo-.
