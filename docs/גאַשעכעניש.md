גאַשעכעניש
'monster'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Corruption of געשעעניש? Max Weinreich suggests it may go back to OHG (perhaps because of the prefix גאַ, as in early OHG ga-, modern Yiddish גע־).
[M. Weinreich, Geshikhte, 4: 115]
