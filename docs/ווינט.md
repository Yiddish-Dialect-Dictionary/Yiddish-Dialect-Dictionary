ווינט
דער
'wind'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Got. winds, OND vindr, OE OS wind, OHG wint < IE h₂u̯eh₁-nt-ó-, vrddhi derivation from the present participle of *h₂u̯eh₁- 'to blow,' cf. Latin ventus, Sanskrit vā́ta-
{Casaretto 60}
The idiom !גיי זוך דעם ווינט אין פֿעלד appears to be a calque of Polish szukaj wiatru w polu.
{Fliciński 406}
