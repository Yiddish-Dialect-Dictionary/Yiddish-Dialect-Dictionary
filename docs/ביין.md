ETYMOLOGY
===========
ON bein, OE bān, English bone, OS bēn, OHG bein, MHG bein, NHG Bein < PGmc. *baina- 'bone, leg' < PIE, from the root *bʰh₂-oi-no-, from the root *bʰeh₂- 'to shine'
Derived from the root to ON beinn 'straight'
The original meaning was 'bone', but already in OHG extended to the whole 'leg' - a meaning that was lost in Eastern Yiddish.
{AHDW 1: 846; BMZ 1: 100; DW² 4: 777; Kluge/Seebold 2011: 105; Kroonen 2013: 48}
