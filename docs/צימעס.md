WESTERN
========

siməs {HOLLAND, Harderwijk 52055, Beem}
ssimmes {HOLLAND, Beem, Jerosche}
	•	Gei ich uf de mark mit leffel, essen se ssimes, gei ich uf de mark mit gobbels, essen se sop. 'If I go to the market with spoons, they are eating tsimes, if I go to the market with forks, they are eating soup. Mij loopt het altijd tegen. Compare the anecdote of the Jewish philosopher, language scholar and exegete Ibn Ezra, who said of himself: If I dealt in burial shrouds, nobody would die anymore; if I were to sell candles, the sun would not set. Ssimmes (older Zimmes) - etymologically difficult to explain: zuo-müese? Z'imbiss?  Gerson 103, Mieses Jidd. Sprache 207.' {Jerosche №605}
