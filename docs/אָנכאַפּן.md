אָנכאַפּן
אָנגעכאַפּט

ETYMOLOGY
===========
The idiom אָנכאַפּן גאָט פֿאַר די פֿיס 'to grab God by the feet (i.e., to be happy over success)' appears to be a calque of Polish  chwycić / trzymać / złapać Pana Boga za nogi.
[Fliciński 55, 427, 531]
