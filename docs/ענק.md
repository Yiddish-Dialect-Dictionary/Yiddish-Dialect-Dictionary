ענק
'you (pl. accusative/dative)'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Got. igqis (dual), ON ykkr (dual), Modern Icelandic ykkur (plural meaning), OE inc (dual; dative), incit (dual; accusative), OS ink (dual), MHG Bavarian enk (plural meaning), NHG Bavarian enk (plural meaning). The diffusion of the pronoun עץ/ענק/ענקער exclusively in Central Yiddish probably indicates a relatively late migration of Jews into Poland from Austrian lands where the Bavarian dialect was spoken. For example, all Jews were expelled from the Duchy of Austria in 1420-21, and again in 1669-70. 
{DWB 3, 483, Feist 291, Kroonen 2013: 276, de Vries 677, M. Weinreich, Geshikhte 4: 123}
"It is conceivable that the Standard Yiddish alternant ajx had already covered the entire area before the spread of enk supplanted it in the west." {Indeed, this seems very likely.}
{Herzog 1964: 76}
