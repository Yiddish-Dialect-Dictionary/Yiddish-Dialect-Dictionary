פֿערד
־
דאָס
(NORTHEASTERN דער, SOUTHEASTERN partly דער, partly דאָס, CENTRAL דאָס)
'horse'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
The comparison האָרעווען ווי אַ פֿערד appears to be a calque of Polish harować jak koń.
{Fliciński 321}
