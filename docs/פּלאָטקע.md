ETYMOLOGY
===========
Belarusian плотка {or плётка?}
Polish plotka, from the verb pleść 'to plait, weave'; from Proto-Slavic *plesti, from PIE *pleḱ- 'to fold, plait, weave'; cognate with Latin plectō, German flechten (see פֿלעכטן)
