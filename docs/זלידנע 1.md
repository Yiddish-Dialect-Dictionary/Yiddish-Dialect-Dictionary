זלידנע 1
'bothersome, annoying' (adjective)

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Polish złydnia 'evil spirit'; from Ukrainian зли́день 'pauper', зли́дні 'poverty; from Proto-Slavic *zъlъ 'bad' (cf. Polish zły). 
[Derksen 2008: 551; Vasmer/Trubachev 299]
