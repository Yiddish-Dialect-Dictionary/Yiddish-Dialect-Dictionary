ETYMOLOGY
===========
Belor. зашпільваць (zašpiĺvać)
Herzog suggests that פֿאַרשפּיליען is "an innovation of eastern origin." In western EY the term used is פֿאַר)קנעפּלען).
{Herzog 1964: 85}

![](https://ia902902.us.archive.org/9/items/Yiddish-Dialect-Maps/Herzog3-25-BorderAreaOverfloodedOrUnapproachedByInnovations-62.jpg)
