מאָטלען
געמאָטלט
'to gossip'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
M. Weinreich proposed that the word was from MHG martern, marteln 'to torment'.
{Herzog 1964: 75}
Cf. Rhineland - Low Franconian moteln (variant of marteln, martern) 'to torment'.
{Rheinisches Wörterbuch 5: 1312}
Polish motać 'reel, tangle'; Ukrainian мотати. 
{Geller 2001: 72}
The /l/ was introduced due to consonant dissimilation.
{Beider 2015: 441}
