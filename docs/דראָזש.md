דראָזש
דראָשטש, דרעשטעס, דרעשטשעס, דריזשקעס
'the chills'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Polish dreszcz 'shivering'; Ukrainian дрож 'tremble'; Russian дрожь; Belarusian дрыжыкі; from Proto-Slavic *drъžь; from Proto-Balto-Slavic *drugjos; cf. Lithuanian drugỹs 'fever'.
