וואָרט

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Got. waúrd, ON orð, OE OS word, OHG wort < PGmc. *wurdaⁿ / *wurð-a- < PIE *u̯r̥dʰ-ó- or (h₂)urdʰ-o, cf. Old Prussian wirds 'word,' Latin verbum, Lithuanian var̃das 'name'
{Bammesberger 1990: 35; Casaretto 87-8; Feist 554; Kroonen 2013: 600}
The idiom האַלטן עמעצן בײַם וואָרט 'to take someone at his word' might be a calque of Polish trzymać kogoś za słowo.
{Fliciński 429}
The idiom גלייבן עמעצן אויפֿן וואָרט 'to take someone's word for it' appears to be a calque of Polish uwierzyć / wierzyć komuś na słowo.
{Fliciński 442, 457}
