שׂרה
'Sarah (woman's name)'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Max Weinreich posits that the שׂ was in early Old Yiddish pronounced as a dorsal /s/, which was sometimes reflected as the letter צ, e.g. diminutive forms of שׂרה from the thirteenth century like צערלין  and צורלין. 
{M. Weinreich, Geshikhte, 2: 37}
