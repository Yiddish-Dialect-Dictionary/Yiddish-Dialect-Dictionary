וועלטלעך
'secular'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Appears to be a calque of Polish świecki 'secular, worldly'.
The idiom וועלטלעכער מענטש 'worldly person, man of the world' appears to be a calque of Polish światowy człowiek.
