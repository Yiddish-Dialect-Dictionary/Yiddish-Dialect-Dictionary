ווילדעווען
'to rage'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
A Germanic root (see ווילד) with the Slavic suffixe -eve-. The verbal suffix ־עווע־ is derived from the Polish suffixes -owa-, -ewa-, -ywa-, -iwa-.
[Geller 2023: 116]
