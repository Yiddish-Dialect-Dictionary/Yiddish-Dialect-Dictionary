הויזן
די
'pants'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Vowel 42, Proto-Yiddish *ō
{Katz 1982: 59}
The idiom אָנמאַכן אין די הויזן 'to poop one's pants (i.e., for fear)' appears to be a calque of Polish narobić w portki (ze strachu).
{Fliciński 224}
