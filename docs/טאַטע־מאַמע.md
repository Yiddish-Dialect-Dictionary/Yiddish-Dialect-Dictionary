טאַטע־מאַמע
די
'parents'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Max Weinreich suggests that pairings of this type (like זיידע־באָבע and חתן־כּלה) may go back to the pattern found in OHG sunufatarungo 'son and father'.
{M. Weinreich, Geshikhte, 4: 115}
