אַנטדעקן
'to discover'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
The idiom אַנטדעקן אַמעריקע 'discover America (i.e., say something that is commonly known already)' might be a calque of Polish odkryć/odkrywać (na nowo) Amerykę.
