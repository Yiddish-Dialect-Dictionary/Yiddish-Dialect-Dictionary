מיר 2
'me (dative)'
{In NORTHEASTERN also used instead of the accusative form מיך.}

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Got. mis, ON mér, OE mē, English (oblique) me, OS (oblique) mī, Dutch (oblique) mij, OHG mir, NHG mir < PGmc. *me-z < PIE *(e)me- + a case ending indifferent to number.
{Kluge/Seebold 2011: 625; Kroonen 2013: 116}
