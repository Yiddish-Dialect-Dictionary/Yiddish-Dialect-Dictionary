פֿאַנג
דער
'catch, haul, capure'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Got. ga-fahs, ON fengr, OE feng 'grasp, span, hug', Dutch vang, OHG fang, MHG vanc, NHG Fang < PGmc. *fangi- 'catch' (DRV), an i-stem derived from *fanhan- (see פֿאַנגען).
