פּאָל 1
־ן
דער
'floor'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Ukrainian пол 'sleeping bench', Belarusian пол 'floor'; Russian пол 'floor', Old Russian полъ 'foundation' < *pol-o-
Related: OE fala 'board' < PGmc. *falan- 'board' (NEUR).
Old Russian полица 'shelf', Ukrainian поли́ця, Polish (archaic, dialect) polica (see פּאָליצע) < Proto-Slavic *polìca 'shelf'.
Old Church Slavonic palica 'staff', Russian па́лица 'club, cudgel', Polish (dialect) palica 'club' < Proto-Slavic *palica 'stick, staff'.
Russian па́лка 'stick, staff', Polish pałka < Proto-Slavic *palъka. 'stick, staff'.
