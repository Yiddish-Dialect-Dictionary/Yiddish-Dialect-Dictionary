טערקיש
'Turkish'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Max Weinreich speculated that the phrase אָפּטאָן אויף טערקיש 'to play a dirty trick on' came from the time of Turkish-Tatar attacks on Ukrainian territory. {M. Weinreich, Geshikhte, 1: 332-333}
