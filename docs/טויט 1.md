טויט 1
'dead' (adjective)

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Got. dauþs, ON dauðr, OE dēad, English dead, OS dōd, Dutch dood, OHG tōt, MHG tôt, German tot < PGmc. *dauda- 'dead' < *dʰou(H)-tó- (DRV); the original past participle to *daujan- (English to die, MHG touwen); cf. Latin fūnus 'funeral', Hittite tuḫḫušzi 'to end'
{DW¹ 21: 588; Kluge/Seebold 2011: 922; Kroonen 2013: 90}
