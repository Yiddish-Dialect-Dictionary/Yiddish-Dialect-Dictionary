לעבער
־ס
די
(NORTHEASTERN דער)
'liver'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
The idiom טראָגן אויף דער לעבער 'to carry on your liver (i.e., to have a concern)' appears to be related to Polish leżeć na wątrobie 'to lie on one's liver (to cause concern, unease)'
