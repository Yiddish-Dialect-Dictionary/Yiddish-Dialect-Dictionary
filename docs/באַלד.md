באַלד
'immediately'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Got. balþs* 'bold, frank', ON ballr 'dangerous, dire', OE beald 'brave', English bold, OHG bald 'bold, strong, intence', baldo (adverb), MHG balde, NHG bald 'soon' < PGmc. *balþ/da- 'brave' (GM) - theoretically from PIE *bʰal-, but nothing to compare it to.
The shift in meaning in German and Yiddish was from 'bold' to 'fast' to 'soon'.
{Kluge/Seebold 2011: 84; Kroonen 2013: 50}
