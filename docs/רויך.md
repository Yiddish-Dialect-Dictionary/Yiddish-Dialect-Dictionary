ETYMOLOGY
===========
Vowel 44, Proto-Yiddish *ɔu
{Katz 1982: 61}
The idiom אַוועק(גיין) מיטן רויך 'to disappear with the smoke' appears to be a calque of Polish coś poszło z dymem.
{Fliciński 313}
