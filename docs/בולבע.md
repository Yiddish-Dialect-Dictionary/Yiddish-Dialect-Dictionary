'potato'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Belarusian бу́льба 'potato'; Ukrainian бу́льба; Lithuanian bulvė; from Polish bulwa, (older) bulba; from Latin bulbus 'bulb (e.g., onion)' (whence English bulb); from Greek βολβός 'onion' ("expressive, sound symbolic, with a kind of reduplication," according to Beekes).
{Bańkowski 1: 94. Beekes 2010: 225. WSJP "bulwa"}
