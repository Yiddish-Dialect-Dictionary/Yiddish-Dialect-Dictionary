{Maißim un Schnokes vun e Handelewos, LEIPZIG 1845: 46}

haan, han {HOLLAND, Beem, Jerosche}
	•	Ibber e hien un a haan. Is es beis hamikdesch chorew gegân (Var. gegangen). 'The temple was destroyed over a chicken and a rooster. Small things have major consequences.' {Jerosche №337}
	•	Wo es hien kreit vor dem han, dou is kaan brooche an. 'Where the woman is the boss, it is no good. Someimes: there is not much to be earned there.' {Jerosche №338}
