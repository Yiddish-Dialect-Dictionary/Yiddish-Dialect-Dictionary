בראָוואַרניע
'brewery'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Polish browarnia, from browar; from MHG broawer 'brewer', from broawen 'to brew'; cf. English  brew, brewer, brewery.
{Bańkowski 79; Brückner 42}
