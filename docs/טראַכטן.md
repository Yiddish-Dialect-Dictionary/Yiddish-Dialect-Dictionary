טראַכטן
'think'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
The idiom טראַכטן אויף אַ קול 'to think out loud' appears to be a calque of Polish myśleć na głos. Similarly, טראַכטן הויך 'to think out loud' appears to be a calque of Polish głośno myśleć.
{Fliciński 109, 212}
