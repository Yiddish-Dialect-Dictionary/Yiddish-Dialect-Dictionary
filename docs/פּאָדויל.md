פּאָדויל
־ן
דער
'plain at the foot of a mountain, piedmont'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Beider argues that the word must have been borrowed from Old Polish before the 16th c. while the vowel of podół was still long /oː/.
Polish podół. 
[Beider 2015: 446]
