מישטיינס געזאָגט
אומשטיינס געזאָגט {variants}
'(expressing regret or disdain)'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Originally an expression of regret (and unironic), derived from an expression intended to ward off evil דעם שטיין זײַ עס געזאָגט 'may it (the bad news) be told to the rock'
{Herzog 1964: 92}
