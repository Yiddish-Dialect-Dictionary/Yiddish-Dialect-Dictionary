ETYMOLOGY
===========
OS bilithi, Dutch beeld, OHG biladi, biledi, bilidi, bilodi, MHG bilde,  NHG Bild 'image, likeness' < West Germanic *bil-eþja-.
The oldest meaning was 'model'
From the root *bil- 'equal, even' (see ביליק)
