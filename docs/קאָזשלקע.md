ETYMOLOGY
===========
Polish koziołek 'little goat; somersault'; from kozioł 'billy goat'; from Proto-Slavic *kozь̀lъ 'he-goat' (cf. Belarusian казёл; Ukrainian козе́л; Russian козёл); from *kozà 'she-goat' (see קאָזע).
