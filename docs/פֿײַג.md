פֿײַג
־ן

'fig'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
The idiom אַ פֿײַג אונטער דער נאָז 'a fig under (their) nose (i.e., nothing)' is a calque of Polish figę pod nosem (itself recorded as פֿיגע פּאָד נאָסעם).
The idiom ווײַזן עמעצן אַ פֿײַג 'show someone a fig (i.e., thumb one's nose)' appears to be a calque of Polish pokazać komuś figę.
