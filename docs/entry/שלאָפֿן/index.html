שלאָפֿן
‘to sleep’

שלאָפֿן
געשלאָפֿן
'to sleep'

ETYMOLOGY

I.
Protovowel A2.
{Katz 1982: 59}
II.

III. Got. slepan, OE slǣpan ~ slāpan, OS slāpan, OHG slāfan; from PGmc ✱slēpanaⁿ

IV. The comparison שלאָפֿן ווי אַ געהרגעטער 'to sleep like a dead man (i.e., soundly)' appears to be a calque of Polish spać jak zabity.
