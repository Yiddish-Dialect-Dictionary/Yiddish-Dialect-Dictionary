Holland (Netherlands)

Kalle [HOLLAND, Beem, Jerosche]
- Die kalle hot potter genascht. 'Said when it rains on the wedding day.' {Jerosche №422}
J.J.V. 1923, 369 - Es reg'nt, de Kalle is e Nascherin.
M.J.V. 1899 blz. 43 - Regen bei der chupe is de Kale e Näscherin.
Bernst. 111 - As chusson-kalu senen naschers, regent bei sej zü der chüpu.
(A widespread folk superstition)
Bernst. 40 - As dus brojt falt mit der püter arup (oder: auf dem punim), is a ssimon, as die kalu is hüngerig. (The connection is not entirely clear.)
- Die kalle is (im) zu schein. 'There is a snake in the grass. The case is presented too nicely. He is too picky. {Jerosche №423}
Bernst. 109 - A chisurejn, die kalu is zü schejn.
Weill 391.
- Die kalle von purem sitzt un schpielt uf. 'She sits and washes ... She has temporarily played the role of bride in the Purim play. Now ordinary life starts again. (ufschpielen = washing barrels.)' {Jerosche №424}
- Man ken sisch amusieren, aach wen man nit mit der kalle tanzt. 'One should not set requirements that are too high.' {Jerosche №425}
- Mitlerein wert m'n tochter de kalle. 'In the middle (suddenly) my daughter becomes the bride. Suddenly entering into an important event. A marriage in the past was very carefully prepared by the parents of both parties.' {Jerosche №426}
- Wen man die kalle zur choppe toot fieren, schtein die meloochem hinter die tieren. 'Relates to the custom that blood relations lead bride and groom under the bridal canopy. {Jerosche №427}
Bernst. 134 - Auf ale Kalojss rüht di sch'chinu.
