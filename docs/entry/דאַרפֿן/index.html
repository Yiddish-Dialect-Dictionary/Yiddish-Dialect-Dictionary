דאַרפֿן
‘to need’

דאַרפֿן
געדאַרפֿט/געדאָרפֿן
'to need'

ETYMOLOGY

Why the vowel /a/?
︀︀Got. þaúrban, ON þurfa, OE þurfan 'to be wanton', OS thurƀan, OHG durfan, MHG durfen, dürfen; from PGmc ✱ þurfan-; from PIE ✱trp-, root of ✱terp- 'to sate oneself'; cf. Sanskrit tŕ̥pyati 'to delight, satisfy', Greek τέρπω 'to satisfy, refresh', Lithuanian tar̃pti 'to thrive', Russian терпеть 'to endure'
