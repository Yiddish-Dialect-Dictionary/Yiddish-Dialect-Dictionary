מיר 2
'me' (dative)

ETYMOLOGY

Got. mis, ON mér, OE mē, English (oblique) me, OS (oblique) mī, Dutch (oblique) mij, OHG mir, NHG mir; from PGmc ✱me-z; from PIE ✱(e)me- + a case ending indifferent to number.
