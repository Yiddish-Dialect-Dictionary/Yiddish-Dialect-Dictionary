בוים
‘tree’

בוים
ביימער
דער
'tree'

ETYMOLOGY

Got. bagms 'tree', ON baðmr 'tree; beam', OE bēam 'tree, pillar, beam, post', English beam, OS bōm, OHG boum, MHG boum, NHG Baum < West Germanic ✱baumaz; from PGmc ✱bagma- 'beam, tree'; from PIE bʰh₂ǵʰ-mo-
Probably related to PGmc ✱bōgu- 'shoulder, upper arm' (e.g., OE bōg 'shoulder, arm; branch', English bough)
{DW² 4: 327;Kluge/Seebold 2011: 97; Kroonen 2013: 47}
Vowel 44, Proto-Yiddish ✱ɔu
{Katz 1982: 61}
