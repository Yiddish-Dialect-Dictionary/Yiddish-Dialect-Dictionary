הייסן
‘to be called; to command’

הייסן
געהייסן
'to be called; to command'

ETYMOLOGY

Got haitan, ON heita, OE hātan, OS hētan, OHG heiʒan, MHG heiʒen, NHG heissen; from PGmc ✱haitan- 'to call'; from PIE ✱k̂oid-, from the root ✱k̂ei-/k̂i-; cf. Latin ciēre 'to move, put in motion', citus 'fast'
