לייענען
געלייענט
'to read'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Western Loez (Gallo-Romance -- pre-Old French!) < Latin legere 'to gather, collect' < PIE *leĝ-
The root vowel appears to be MHG ei (E₄).
{M. Weinreich, Geshikhte, 1: 112, 2: 62, 4: 87-88}

WESTERN
========

לייאן is the dominant form in Old Yiddish; the extended form לייענען first appears in a document from Vilnius from 1563 {M. Weinreich, Geshikhte, 2: 62, 4: 87}
