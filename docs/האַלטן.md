האַלטן
געהאַלטן
'hold'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
A11
The idiom האַלטן אין שאַך 'to keep in check' appears NOT to be a calque of Polish trzymać kogoś w szachu, which actually means  'to hold someone in the palm of one's hand'. Rather it agrees with NHG in Schach halten 'to keep in check'.
{Fliciński 429}
The idiom האַלטן עמעצן בײַם וואָרט 'to take someone at his word' might be a calque of Polish trzymać kogoś za słowo.
{Fliciński 429}
