ETYMOLOGY
===========
ON bekkr, OE benc, English bench, OS bank, OHG banc, MHG banc, NHG Bank < PGmc. *banki- 'bench' (NEUR)
Perhaps rom *bankan- 'slope, cliff'.
The original gender in German was masculine, became feminine in MHG.
