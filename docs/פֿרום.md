פֿרום

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Proto-Eastern Yiddish /frum/ stems from the Upper German variant in /u/ <frumm> (similarly קומען, גענומען, געשוווּמען).
{M. Weinreich, Geshikhte, 2: 85-86}
Vowel 51, Proto-Yiddish *u
{Katz 1982: 58}
