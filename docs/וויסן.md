וויסן
געוווּסט
'to know'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Got. 2 sg. pres. ind. wáist, ON veizt < PGmc. *waiss (with later re-addition of the 2 sg. pret. inflection -t) < wait-t < PIE *u̯oi̯d-th₂e
{Fulk 322}
The idiom איין גאָט ווייס 'God only knows, who knows' appears to be a calque of Polish Bóg jeden wie.
