זיידע־באָבע
די
'grandparents'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Max Weinreich suggests that pairings of this type (like טאַטע־מאַמע and חתן־כּלה) may go back to the pattern found in OHG sunufatarungo 'son and father'.
[M. Weinreich, Geshikhte, 4: 115]
