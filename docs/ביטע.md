ביטע
'please'
{GERMANISM}

ETYMOLOGY
===========
NHG bitte, an ellipsis of ich bitte (dich) 'I ask you'. {The cognate to NHG bitten was lost in Eastern Yiddish, replaced by the cognate to NHG beten (see בעטן). In Yiddish the verb would have lost the final -e.}
