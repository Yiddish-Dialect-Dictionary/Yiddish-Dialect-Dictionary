וואַטן־מאַכער
דער
'the Devil'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
In Central Yiddish טײַוול is pronounced /taːvl/, which was deformed to וואַטל, which looked like a diminutive of וואַטע 'cotton'; וואַטל־מאַכער was then deformed to וואַטן־מאַכער {M. Weinreich, Geshikhte, 3: 199}
