נחום
נַחוּם 
'Nahum (man's name)'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
The original patach in an open syllable was lengthened in the late Middle Ages and shifted to /o/, just like long MHG â (A₂) and lengthened MHG a (A₃).
[M. Weinreich, Geshikhte, 2: 44]
