ETYMOLOGY
===========
Polish rymarz; from NHG Riemer; from MHG riemære.
{Brückner 472; Herzog 1964: 112}

![](https://ia902902.us.archive.org/9/items/Yiddish-Dialect-Maps/Herzog3-56-59-ManeTurnerSaddlerCarpenter-82.jpg)
Curious that the distribution of סטאָליער and סטאָליאַר with a final /r/ does not match the similar situation of טאָקער and רימער. The form /rima/ is strange because it mostly occurs in territory where r-dropping does not usually occur.
