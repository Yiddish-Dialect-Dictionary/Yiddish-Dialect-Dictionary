געשמייכל

WESTERN
========

Geschmeichel {HOLLAND, Beem, Jerosche}
	•	Viel Geschmeichel, wenig sseichel. Var. Harbe schmeichel, winzig sseichel. 'Said of a person who laughs at the most insignificant things, specially a dumb girl.' {Jerosche №256}
