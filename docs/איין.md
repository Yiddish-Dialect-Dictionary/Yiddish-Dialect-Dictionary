איין
'one'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Got. ains, ON einn, OE ān, English one, OS ēn, OHG ein, NHg ein < PGmc. *aina- < PIE Hoi-Hn-o; cf Latin ūnus, Lithuanian víenas, Russian inój 'different, other', Sanskrit éka-, Greek οἶος 'alone'
{Burrow 258; Kroonen 2013: 11}
The idiom איין גאָט ווייס 'God only knows, who knows' appears to be a calque of Polish Bóg jeden wie.
