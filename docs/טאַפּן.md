טאַפּן
געטאַפּט
'to feel'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Norwegian dabba 'to beat, kick', English to dab, ENHG tappen, dappen, NHG tappen, (Palatinate) tapfen, tappen 'to grope', (Swiss) tâpen, täppen < PGmc. *dapp/bōn- 'to beat' < *dʰh₂bʰ-néh₂- (NEUR), from the Germanic-Baltic root *dʰeh₂bʰ-; cf. Lithuanian dóbti 'to beat, kill'.
Not documented in MHG before the 15th c.
Or perhaps from NHG Tappe 'paw' < late MHG tâpe (onomatopoeic, or secondary full-grade).
