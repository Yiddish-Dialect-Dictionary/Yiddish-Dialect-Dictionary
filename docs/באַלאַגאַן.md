באַלאַגאַן

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Polish bałagan 'fairground stall (19th c.); disorder, chaos (20th c.)'; borrowed from Russian балага́н 'mess, chaos', from a Turkish language, from Persian bâlâxâne 'upper room'.
