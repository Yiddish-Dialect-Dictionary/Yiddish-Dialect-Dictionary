ETYMOLOGY
===========
Polish brzytwa; Ukrainian бри́тва; Russian бри́тва; from Proto-Slavic *brìtva, a verbal derivative in *-tva of *brìti 'to shave'; from PIE *bʰreyH- 'to cut'.
[Derksen 2008: 63]
