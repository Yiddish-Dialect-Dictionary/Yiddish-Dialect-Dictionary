ביטער
'bitter'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
ON bitr 'biting, sharp; bitter', OE bitor, bitter, bittor, English bitter, OS bittar, Dutch bitter, OHG bittar 'bitter, harsh, spicy', NHG bitter < PGmc. *bitra- 'bitter, sharp' (DRV).
The OHG -t- was not shifted to -z- before -r-
A ra-stem created to *bītan-. Got. baitrs is a ro-stem. 
{Kluge/Seebold 2011: 127; Kroonen 2013: 66}
