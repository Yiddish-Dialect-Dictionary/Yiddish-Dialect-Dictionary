וועג
־ן

'way, path, road'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Got. wigs, ON vegr, OE weg, English way, OS weg, OHG weg, NHG Weg < PGmc. *wega- 'way, road'. Derived from the strong verb *wegan- 'to move, carry' (see באַוועגן).
{Feist 563, Kroonen 2013: 577}
