שיקן
געשיקט

ETYMOLOGY
===========
{Is the meaning 'to send' only from NHG?}
MHG schicken 'to prepare, order', originally from Low German; apparently an intensive construction to געשען.
{Kluge/Seebold 2011: 803}

========
