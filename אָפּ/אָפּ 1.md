אָפּ 1
'off; down (adverb)'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Why is the consonant devoiced /p/?
Got. af, ON af, OE æf, of, OS af, OHG aba, ab. Cf. Latin ab, Greek ἀπό, Sanskrit āpa, Old Church Slavonis по 'after'
{Magnússon 3, de Vries 2}

WESTERN
========
