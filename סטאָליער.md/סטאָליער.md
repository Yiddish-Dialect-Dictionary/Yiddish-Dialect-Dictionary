ETYMOLOGY
===========
Polish stolarz (since the 15th c., a calque of German Tischler); Belarusian  сталя́р. 
{Boryś 579; Brückner 517; Herzog 1964: 112}

![](https://ia902902.us.archive.org/9/items/Yiddish-Dialect-Maps/Herzog3-56-59-ManeTurnerSaddlerCarpenter-82.jpg)
Curious that the distribution of סטאָליער and סטאָליאַר with a final /r/ does not match the similar situation of טאָקער and רימער.
