ETYMOLOGY
===========
{Whence the NEY variant {šam}?}
MHG schûm, schoum. 
Herzog suggests that the NEH vowel was formerly {au} and that the {u} offglide was assimilated by the following labial {m}.
{Herzog 1964: 271, 274}
