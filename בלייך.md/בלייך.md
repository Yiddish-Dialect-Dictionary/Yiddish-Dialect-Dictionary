ETYMOLOGY
===========
ON bleikr, OE blāc, OS blēk, Dutch bleek, OHG bleih, MHG bleich, NHG bleich < PGmc. *blaika- 'pale' (DRV), from the root *bʰleiǵ-; an o-grade adjective created to the verb *blīkan- 'to shine', MHG blīken (see בלישטשען).
