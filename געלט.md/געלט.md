ETYMOLOGY
===========
Got. gild, ON gjald, OE gield, OS geld, OHG gelt < PGmc. *ǥelđ-a-, e-grade derivation from *ǥelđa- 'entgelten'
The idiom וואַרפֿן געלט אין בלאָטע 'to throw money in the mud (i.e., to waste money)' appears to be a calque of Polish wyrzucić/wyrzucać pieniądze w błoto.
{Fliciński 491}
The idiom אָננעמען פֿאַר גוט געלט 'to take for good money (i.e., to take at face value)' appears to be a calque of Polish przyjąć / przyjmować coś za dobrą monetę coś za dobrą monetę. 
{Fliciński 335}
