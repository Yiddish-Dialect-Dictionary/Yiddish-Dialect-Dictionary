מאָרגן
'morning, tomorrow'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
The idiom מאָרגן איז אויך אַ טאָג 'tomorrow is also a day (i.e., something can be done later)' may be a calque of Polish jutro też jest dzień. 
{Fliciński 145}
