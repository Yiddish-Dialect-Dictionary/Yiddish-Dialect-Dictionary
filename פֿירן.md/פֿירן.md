ETYMOLOGY
===========
ON fœra, OE fēran 'to go', OS fōrian, Dutch voeren, OHG fuorran, fuoren, fuorôn, MHG vüeren, vuoren, NHG führen < PGmc. *fōrjan- 'to lead' (or *fōr-eja-) (DRV); the causative to *faran- (see פֿאָרן).
{DW² 9: 1206; Kluge/Seebold 2011: 322; Kroonen 2013: 151}
