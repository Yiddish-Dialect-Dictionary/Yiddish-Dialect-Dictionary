הויך 1
העכער
העכסט
'high, tall'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
The meaning 'tall' appears to be a calque of Polish wysoki 'high, tall'.
The idiom טראַכטן הויך 'to think out loud' appears to be a calque of Polish głośno myśleć.
{Fliciński 109}
