זײַנוול
זאַנוול, זאַמוול, זאַוול
'Zaynvl (man's name)'

ETYMOLOGY
===========
Hebrew שמואל. Max Weinreich posits that the initial ש was pronounced with an apical /s̺/ in early Old Yiddish which subsequently became /z/. However, זײַנוול likely came by way of MHG Samuel, which, in any case, also had an apical /s̺/.
{Beider 2001, 419, M. Weinreich, Geshikhte, 2: 37}
