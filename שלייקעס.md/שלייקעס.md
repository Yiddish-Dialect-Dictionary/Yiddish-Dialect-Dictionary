ETYMOLOGY
===========
{But whence the more eastern form שלייקעס? Polish dialect?}
Polish szelki (since the 16th c.), (dialectal) szolki, a diminutive of szla 'harness'; from Western Slavic *šьľa; borrowed from OHG silo 'belt, strap'.
