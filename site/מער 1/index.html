    מער 1 'more'

    ETYMOLOGY

    Vowel 25, Proto-Yiddish *ɛ {Katz 1982: 60} The expression וואָס אַ מאָל מער 'even more' is a calque of Polish co raz więcej. {Gajek 2023a: 15}

    WESTERN

    meer {Sprach-Meister 1742}

    meː {SWITZERLAND, Fleischer 2005}
