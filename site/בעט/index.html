    בעט ־ן דאָס (NORTHEASTERN די = intermediate gender) 'bed'

    ETYMOLOGY

    Got. badi, ON beðr, OE bed, bedd, English bed, OS bed, beddi OHG betti, MHG bette, bet(t), NHG Bett, Beet 'flower bed' < PGmc. badja- 'bed, bedding' < PIE bʰodʰ-io-; neuter ja-stem from root *bʰedʰ- 'to poke, dig'; cf. Latin fodiō 'dig', fossa 'pit', Greek βόθρος 'pit', Lithuanian badýti 'to poke' {BMZ 1: 109; DW² 5: 17; EWA 1: 572; Feist 73; Kroonen 2013: 46}

    WESTERN
