    שוין

    ETYMOLOGY

    Vowel 42, Proto-Yiddish *ō {Katz 1982: 59} The phrase און שוין 'and that's it' is a calque of Polish i już 'idem'. {Gajek 2023b: 252} Similarly, the negation שוין ניט is a loan translation of Polish już nie 'no more, not anymore, no longer'. שוין מער ניט is from Polish już więcej nie 'no more, not anymore'. {Geller and Gajek 2023: 76}
