    זען געזען 'see'

    ETYMOLOGY

    Got. saíƕan, ON sjā, OE sēon, English to see, OS sehan, OHG sehan, NHG sehen < PGmc. sehwan- 'to see' < PIE sékʷ-e- 'to follow'; cf. Latin sequor, sequī, Sanskrit sācate, Greek ἕπομαι {Feist 404, Kroonen 2013: 431} Vowel 25, Proto-Yiddish *ɛ {Katz 1982: 60}
