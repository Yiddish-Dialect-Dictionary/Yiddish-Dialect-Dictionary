    לאָזן געלאָזן ~ געלאָזט

    ETYMOLOGY

    Got. letan, ON láta, OE lǣtan, OS lāatan, OHG lāʒan < PGmc. lētanaⁿ 'to let go, to allow' {Ringe & Taylor 11} Vowel 41, Proto-Yiddish ɔ {Katz 1982: 58}

    WESTERN

    גלושן {Cambridge Codex, ca. 1382; Eli Katz (1963) 80ff.} Approximate pronunciation: /gəloːsən/ (rhymes with שטרוסן /stroːsən/) Notable that the vowel (A₂, MHG â) appears already as /o/
