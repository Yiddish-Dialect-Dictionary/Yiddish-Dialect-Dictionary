    זון זין דער 'son'

    ETYMOLOGY

    Got. sunus, ON sunr, sonr, OE sunu, English son, OS sunu, OHG sun(u), NHG Sohn < PGmc. sunu- < PIE suH-nú-, from the root suH- 'to give birth to'; cf. Sanskrit sūnú-, Lithuanian sūnùs, Russian сын {Feist 460; Kroonen 2013: 492} PSY ziːn > SEY zin {Jacobs}
