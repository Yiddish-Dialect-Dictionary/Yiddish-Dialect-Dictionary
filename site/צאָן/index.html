    צאָן ציין/ציינער דער 'tooth'

    ETYMOLOGY

    Got. tunþus, ON tǫnn, OE tōð, OS tand, OHG zan(t) < Germ. tanþ- ~ tunþ- < PIE h₃dont-, from the root h₃ed- 'to bite, be sharp'; cf. Latin dēns, Sanskrit dān, Lithuanian dantìs {Feist 483, Kroonen 2013: 509}
