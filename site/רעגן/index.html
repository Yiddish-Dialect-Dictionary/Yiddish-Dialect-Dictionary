    רעגן ־ס דער 'rain' רעגנדל (diminutive)

    ETYMOLOGY

    The use of פֿאַלן אַ רעגן (rather than רעגענען) may be a calque of Polish padać deszcz.

    Got. rign, ON regn, OE regn, rignan 'to rain', English rain, OS regin, regan, OHG regan, reganōn 'regnen' < PGmc. regna- < EUR? Hrégʰ-no-(?); cf. Latin ir-rigāre 'to irrigate' {Feist 397, Kroonen 2011: 48; Kroonen 2013: 408}
