    נאַכט

    ETYMOLOGY

    PGmc. nahts was an i-stem with a genitive nahts, thus the Yiddish adverbs with נאַכטס like שבת־צו־נאַכטס, צו נאַכטס

    cf. Sanskrit nakt-, Greek νύξ, Latin nox {Burrow 75} Vowel 11, Proto-Yiddish *a {Katz 1982: 58}

    [Yiddish Language Atlas of the Soviet Union 1931, map 6]
