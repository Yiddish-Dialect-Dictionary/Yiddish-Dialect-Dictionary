    זיידע ־ס דער 'grandfather'

    ETYMOLOGY

    Old Czech děda (diminutive, or else a vocative form); the initial consonant was influenced by Old Polish dziad. Beider suggests that intimate family vocabulary is likely to come from the West Cnaanic (Old Czech) substrate. {Beider 2015: 430, 451}
