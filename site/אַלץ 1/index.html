    אַלץ 1 אַלעס 'everything'

    {Yiddish Language Atlas of the Soviet Union 1931, map 73}

    ETYMOLOGY

    The phrase אַלץ איינס '(it's) all the same' appears to be a calque of Polish wszystko jedno.

    WESTERN

    אלס {Mirkeves ha-mishne, Cracow 1534}
