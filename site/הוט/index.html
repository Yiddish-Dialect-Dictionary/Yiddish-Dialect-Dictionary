    הוט היט דער 'hat' היטל (diminutive)

    ETYMOLOGY

    ON hattr, OE hæt, English hat (< PGmc. hatta-); OS hōd 'hat', OE hōd, English hood, OHG huot 'hood, hat' < PGmc. hōþō, hattaz, hadeni (perhaps 'cover made of goatskin') < PIE kéh₂/3t-ōn, kh₂/3t-nós, *kh₂/3t-én-i; cf. Latin cassis 'helmet'; or perhaps Latin catulus 'young animal' {Kroonen 2011: 304}

    WESTERN
