    אַל אַלע, אילע 'all'

    ETYMOLOGY

    {Why the variants with /i/-vowels (ile אילע etc.)? MHG elliu, elle (feminine)} {Lexer 1: 33} Got. alls, ON allr, OE eall, English all, OS OHG al(l), MHG al, NHG all < PGmc. alla- < h₂el-nó- (EUR); cf. Lithuanian aliaĩ 'completely'; the double l in Germanic developed out of the cluster -ln- {EWA 1, 129, Feist 40, Kroonen 2013: 23, de Vries 7}
