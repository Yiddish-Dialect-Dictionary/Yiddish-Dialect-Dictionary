    פֿלעגן געפֿלעגט

    {CYED} הװ איך פֿלעג 1st person sg דו פֿלעגסט 2nd person sg ער/זי פֿלעג(ט) 3rd person sg מיר פֿלעגן 1st person pl איר פֿלעגט 2nd person pl זײ פֿלעגן 3rd person pl auxiliary verb of the frequentative form of the past tense, followed by an infinitive: used to, was/were wont to he used to say, he was in the habit of saying ער פֿלעג(ט) זאָגן

    ETYMOLOGY

    The habitual aspect arose in MHG. {Schäfer 2023: 89}
